Preparing the UV Chamber

Setting up a UV chamber correctly is critical for accurate TLC analysis. Follow these steps:

  1. Choose a stable and dark location for your UV chamber.
  2. Install UV lamps with appropriate wavelengths for your TLC plate.
  3. Ensure no external light sources interfere with the chamber.
  4. Calibrate the chamber by testing with a known TLC plate.

For more detailed instructions, consult the FAQ section.
